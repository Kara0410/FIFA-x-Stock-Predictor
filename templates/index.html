How the Football Model Works

⚔ Attack Index

Goals per match (35%), shots (20%), shots on target (25%), possession (10%) and passing accuracy (10%), each normalized across the 32 knockout teams.

🛡 Defense Index

Clean sheets (25%), inverse goals conceded (35%), tackles & interceptions (15%), goalkeeper/defender contribution (15%) and discipline (10%).

⭐ Player Impact Index

Key-player goals, assists, per-90 contribution, minutes and availability, with a penalty for yellow/red cards.

📈 Momentum / Form Index

Recency-weighted results (newest match counts most), group points and goal difference per match.

🥋 Discipline Index

Fewer fouls, yellow and red cards raise the score. Red cards are weighted hardest.

🎲 Match Probability

Poisson expected goals (xGA = base × attackA / defenseB) converted to win/draw/loss via a full score grid, blended with a logistic strength curve. Knockout draws are resolved by a penalty model tilted 30% toward the stronger side.

⚽ Football Signals

📊 Market Features

Daily returns, 3/5-day moving averages, rolling volatility, momentum and the cumulative trend since the tournament started (2026-06-11).

🤖 Forecast Method

A GradientBoostingRegressor predicts next-day returns and is rolled forward 7 trading days. The shaded band is a ±1.28σ residual cone. With only ~3 weeks of history this is a pipeline demo, not financial advice.
